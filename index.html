VICTORIA HUGHES, HARPIST


A native of the Bay Area, Victoria trained at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music before receiving a full scholarship to the Peabody Conservatory of the Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, where she graduated with her degree in harp under Jeanne Chalifoux


After performing with vocalists and orchestras in Washington, D.C., Victoria was recruited by Princess Cruises to play aboard the “Love Boat,” and eventually became a featured entertainer with her own show. Accompanied by the ship’s orchestra, she performed pop, swing, jazz, and classical styles on the harp. Because of her varied experience she is able to teach all different types of music on the harp.
